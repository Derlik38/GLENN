About GLENN

The Gait Learning and Evaluating via Neural Networking Robot

The Mission

GLENN is an experimental humanoid robot designed to walk, learn, and adapt through neural networks and real-time feedback. The project aims to push the boundaries of robotics through iterative development, blending hardware engineering, AI, and embedded systems.

Meet the Creator

I’m Jonah Moro, a second-year engineering student at Auburn University with a deep passion for robotics, control systems, and AI. From autonomous vehicles to mechanical arms, I’ve been building tech that thinks, moves, and interacts with the world. GLENN is my most ambitious personal project to date.

Collaborators

While I’ve led most of the work on GLENN, I’ve had invaluable support from friends, mentors, and fellow students in areas like CAD modeling, control theory, and machine learning.
