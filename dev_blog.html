Development Blog

July 31, 2025 - First Prototype Walk Test

Today, GLENN took its first real steps! After weeks of tuning the balance control loop and tweaking the gait engine, we managed to get a stable 3-step sequence without external support. We observed a slight drift to the right, likely due to weight imbalance on the left knee actuator. Next steps: recalibrate joint torque estimates and add real-time feedback for lateral correction.

July 18, 2025 - Hip Actuation Upgrade

We replaced the old belt-drive hip actuator with a direct-drive cycloidal system. This drastically improved torque response and simplified the PID loop. Early torque ripple was smoothed by applying low-pass filtering. Expecting better stability in forward movement testing next week.
