Meet GLENN

A Deep Dive into the Humanoid Robot’s Design and Purpose

Overview

GLENN is a humanoid research robot designed for experimentation in balance, locomotion, and autonomous behavior. Its purpose is to test and explore advanced gait strategies and adaptive learning techniques using modern neural networks and real-world feedback.

Hardware

  • High-torque brushless motors with cycloidal gearboxes
  • IMU and force sensors for real-time balancing
  • 3D-printed and CNC aluminum frame components

Software & AI

  • Custom neural network for gait optimization
  • Real-time control system using embedded C++ & Python
  • Simulation and training with MuJoCo + reinforcement learning

Development Timeline

The project began in early 2024 and is expected to continue through multiple phases:

  • Phase 1: Hardware prototyping & joint design (✔️ complete)
  • Phase 2: Balance control & sensor integration (in progress)
  • Phase 3: Walking gait learning & refinement
  • Phase 4: Advanced tasks, obstacle avoidance, and interaction
